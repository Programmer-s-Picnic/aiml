Central Government Services

These services are generally valid across India. They include Aadhaar, PAN, ration card, passport, voter ID, DigiLocker, UMANG, Ayushman Bharat and national service portals.

Help for Central Services

  • For identity services, keep your mobile number ready because OTP may be required.
  • For PAN and income tax services, check name, date of birth and Aadhaar details carefully.
  • For ration card services, the final application usually goes through the state portal.

Uttar Pradesh State Services

These services are focused on Uttar Pradesh. They include electricity bill, UP ration card, land records, property registration, municipality tax, water services and certificates.

Help for UP Services

  • For electricity bills, keep your account number or consumer number ready.
  • For land records, keep district, tehsil, village, khasra or khatauni details ready.
  • For municipality tax or water tax, keep house number, ward number or property ID ready.
